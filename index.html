Frequently Asked Questions

Get answers to common questions about our tools, post-AMA strategies, expected results, and how we can help grow your project across multiple platforms.

  • What platforms or tools do you use to track the performance of the AMA and marketing efforts?

    We use a combination of analytics tools such as Twitter Analytics, Telegram Insights, and third-party platforms like Google Analytics and Hootsuite to track engagement, impressions, and overall performance of the AMA and related marketing efforts.

  • Can you help with post-AMA marketing or follow-ups?

    Yes, we offer post-AMA marketing strategies, including community engagement through follow-up posts, recaps, and targeted campaigns to keep the conversation going and maintain momentum.

  • What type of results should I expect after an AMA session?

    You can expect increased brand visibility, more followers across platforms, engagement from a targeted audience, and potential partnerships or collaborations as a result of the AMA.

  • Do you offer additional services like content creation, giveaways, or influencer outreach?

    Absolutely! We provide content creation services for promotional materials, manage giveaways to increase community participation, and can connect you with influencers for broader outreach.

  • What is your approach to marketing projects on social media?

    Our approach is data-driven and tailored to each platform. On Twitter, we focus on viral content and hashtags, while on Telegram and Discord, we prioritize community interaction and personalized engagement.

  • Can you support project growth on multiple platforms at once?

    Yes, we specialize in multi-platform growth strategies. We ensure a consistent message across Twitter, Telegram, Discord, and any other platform, while customizing content and engagement to fit each audience's preferences.
